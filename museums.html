Museums

One of our most favorite activities when in any city is visiting their museums. This is because museums can be an excellent way to glimpse into the culture, history and style of any place. Addis Ababa is a city overflowing with history and culture, and these are some of our favorite museums to visit, with company, or by yourself!

  • Red Terror Museum

    • We start our list with a very sad part of Ethiopian History, which occured during the Derg regime. The Derg regime is known for being an authoritarian government, and the Red Terror is a dark time in Ethiopian history when the government killed anyone who dared to oppose him. Swing by at this museum to learn more, and for a look into a sad history.

  • National Museum of Ethiopia

    • Proudly displaying one of the first ever humans to be dicovered, Lucy, this museum definitely has an awe-inspiring factor to it from the very start. From ancient relics of various Ethiopian kings(and queens!) to a very wide range of cultural and historical objects, this museum will leave you feeling in awe of all the history this amazing city has to offer.

  • Unity Park Museum

    • This park, located in 4 kilo, is nothing short of an entire city by itself! In addition to the restaurants and various other entertainment it has to offer, this park also has a very well-built and amazing museum. Inside this museum, you will not only find the statue of one of the most popular ruler of Ethiopia, Menilik the Second, but also many of his possessions as well. There are tributes to various other rulers as well. This museum is truly a great way to introduce yourself to Ethiopian and Addis Ababan culture!

Souvenir Shops

Souvenir shops are one of the most important part of any trip. Whether it's to get yourself something to remember the trip by, or whether you want to gift your friends and family something, souvenir shops are where it's at! And here at Discover-R-Us, souvenir shops have our whole hearts! Therefore, we have curated a list of our top spots to get yourself(or your family and friends) something to remember this trip by.

  • Merkato

    • The merkato is 2 sq miles of shops, local manufacturing, of almost every conceivable type of usable item. Wear your walking shoes. Photos are a matter of who you are shooting. Some people enjoy it and even ask for it.This market is very chaotic and fun . It is a must visit place in Addis Ababa to get your souvenirs!

  • Tomoca

    • The coffee there smells like freshly ground heaven. At tomoca you do not only drink coffee , you live the coffee. You can take ground coffee back to your place for you to enjoy later, and this is an excellent gift for all your coffee addicted friends!

  • Bookstore

    • Bookshops are places of endless entertainment and renewal; what's not to love? You can browse books and have a good chat with a friend while you are there, but that's not all. If you have a bookworm friend or you are a fan of books yourself , you can look here if you're thinking of buying a friend or yourself a treat. Go ahead and check this book store out to get yourself or your friends a souvenir!
